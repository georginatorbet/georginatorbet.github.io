Proofreading services

I offer proofreading of English texts, for both commercial and academic purposes. Let me help your report, paper or thesis shine! I correct spelling, grammar, and English phrasing, and in addition I can edit academic texts.

I am a native English speaker (born and educated in Britain), and have been proofreading for private businesses for several months now. I have extensive experience of written work in both science and philosophy, having a BA in Philosophy and an MSc in Neuroscience in addition to my PhD in psychology. I can therefore confidently proofread and edit a broad range of topics, specialising in social and natural sciences.

Examples of academic papers for which I offered corrections:

Wiers, C. (2014). The automatic pull of drug cues in addiction: Neural correlates and effects of re-training (Doctoral dissertation, Freie Universität Berlin, Germany).

Ludwig, V. U., and Simner, J. (2013). What colour does that feel? Tactile–visual mapping and the development of cross-modality. Cortex, 49(4), 1089-1099.

Ludwig, V. (2013). Impulse and self-control: Investigations using functional magnetic resonance imaging and hypnosis (Doctoral dissertation, Fakultät II der Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, Germany).

Wiers, C. E. (2012). Methylation and the human brain: towards a new discipline of imaging epigenetics. European archives of psychiatry and clinical neuroscience, 262(3), 271-273.

Gierhan, S. (2012). Brain networks for language. Anatomy and functional roles of neural pathways supporting language comprehension and repetition. (Doctoral dissertation, Universität Leipzig, Germany).
